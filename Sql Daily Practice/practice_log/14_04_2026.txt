## Learnings

- JOIN direction decides data completeness
- LEFT JOIN keeps main table intact
- NULL means no match in JOIN
- GROUP BY + HAVING used for filtered aggregation
- Correlated subquery compares row with group
- CTE helps break complex logic into steps
